# Code of Conduct

GITSEARCH is an open-source project built on the principles of respect, inclusion and collaboration.

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions
- Welcome contributions from all backgrounds and skill levels
- Focus on what is best for the community and the project

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported to anzizdaouda0@gmail.com.
Maintainers will review and respond within 7 days.
